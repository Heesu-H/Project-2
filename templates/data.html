Victorian Crime Statistics Agency (CSA):

https://discover.data.vic.gov.au/dataset/crime-by-location-data-table

The Crime Statistics Agency (CSA) is responsible for processing, analysing and publishing Victorian crime statistics, independent of Victoria Police. The CSA aims to provide an efficient and transparent information service to assist and inform policy makers, researchers and the Victorian public.

ETL-Project: Crime Data For Greater Melbourne

https://github.com/tomjp90/ETL-Project

This Git repositry was the starting point for our web app. This only includes crime data for suburbs and LGAs for Greater Melbourne. As a result, this required rework of the entire data to include all of Victorias crime and and links between LGAs and suburbs for our visualisation.
Over 30,000 rows of data were summarised in our app!

Victorian Local Government Area Boundary:

https://data.gov.au/dataset/ds-dga-bdf92691-c6fe-42b9-a0e2-a4cd716fa811/details

This is the link to the GEOjson for the LGA boundaries built and maintained by Geoscape Australia using authoritative government data. This is used to create the cloropleth boundaries for the visualisation page.

Leaflet Timesline Slider: Leaflet Plaugin

https://github.com/svitkin/leaflet-timeline-slider

This is a leaflet plugin that creates a customizable timeline slider feature on our visualisation that allows relevant crime values for each year for 2010 - 2020.
