Overview

Need to find a safe place to live in Victoria?

This web app uses data from the Victorian Government website to visualise occurrences of crime within Victorian Local Government Areas (LGAs) to assist finding a safer area to live!

Using the Visualisation page, an interative map is created of Victoria to easily see the lowest crime areas. Higher crime areas are show in a darker red where as lower crime areas are shown in the green spectrum. By clicking c LGA, relevant suburbs can be determined to narrow the scope. Using the time slider at the bottom of the visualisation, crime for 2010 - 2020 can be easily visualised for each of the years.

Exploring the Data page will let you filter down to the area specific to you interests.
